What I have to do when update this theme?
- Update Bootstrap version at inc/BootstrapBasic.php in `registerCommonScripts` and `registerCommonStyles` methods.
- Update Font Awesome version at functions.php in `bootstrapBasicEnqueueScripts` function.
- Update HTML5 Shiv version at functions.php in `bootstrapBasicEnqueueScripts` function.
- Update Respond.js version at functions.php in `bootstrapBasicEnqueueScripts` function.
- Update Modernizr version at functions.php in `bootstrapBasicEnqueueScripts` function.
- Update theme version number at style.css
