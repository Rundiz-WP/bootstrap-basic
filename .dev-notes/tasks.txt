What I have to do when update this theme?
- Update Bootstrap version at inc/BootstrapBasic.php in `registerBootstrapAssets` method.
- Update Font Awesome version at functions.php in `bootstrapBasicEnqueueScripts` function.
- Update Modernizr version at functions.php in `bootstrapBasicEnqueueScripts` function.
- Update theme version number at readme.txt & style.css
- Write change log in changelog.md
